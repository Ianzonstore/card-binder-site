Steps to Use Your Binder

  • Clean your cards before storing them.
  • Use standard sleeves or toploaders.
  • Place the binder on a flat surface.
  • Insert 1 card per pocket (top entry).
  • Avoid overfilling or placing heavy objects.
  • Use dividers or labels to organize.
  • Store away from direct sunlight or humidity.
  • Use a padded bag for events.
